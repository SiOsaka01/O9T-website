Happy Feet Epic Walkers : Nine Trigs Challenge

    The 9TrigChallenge is a 24 hour epic-walking challenge.
    It is a walk to the top of nine of the highest hills in Oldham, UK.
    The goal is to visit each hilltop, take a photo next to the OS-Station, or triangulation (trig point) pillar; within any 24 hour period and post your results on the group walking page.
    The course is rated as difficult and the total distance is in excess of 50 miles (estimated).
    No motor or wheeled vehicles, just walking.
